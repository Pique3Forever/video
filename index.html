Blue Sea

The sea has nothing but blue.

INTRODUCION

        大海，我的故乡。海是忧郁的蓝色，平静、广阔抑或是汹涌、澎湃，没有单一的形容词能描述。海是神秘的蓝色，用深不可测的力量改变着人们的命运：无论风平浪静还是暗潮涌动，海的变化莫测让人心生畏惧。作品将视角锁定在暴雨夜前后的海边，从黑夜到白天，海从咆哮的波塞冬悄无声息变为温和的海民。一切转变都来得如此之快，正如大海本身一样捉摸不透。连夜暴雨被大海收容，清晨慵懒的潮水里早已看不出昨夜的风狂雨骤。发生了什么，只有大海知道。 
       
        Sea, my hometown. The sea is blue. There is no single adjective to describe it. It is calm, broad or turbulent. The sea is a mysterious blue, which changes people's fate with unfathomable power: no matter whether the sea is calm or the tide is surging, the unpredictable changes of the sea make people fear. From night to day, the sea changes from the roaring Poseidon to the gentle sea man. All changes have come so fast, as elusive as the sea itself. The torrential rain in the night was taken in by the sea. The lazy tide in the morning could not show the storm of last night. Only the sea knows what happened.
